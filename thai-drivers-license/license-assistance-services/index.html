Thai Driver's License Assistance Services

The DLT process is entirely manageable if you have the right documents and know what to expect. For those who would rather have someone experienced handle the paperwork, appointments and DLT navigation on their behalf, a number of professional services exist specifically for foreign applicants. Here are the most trusted options by region.

Bangkok — BSR Bike Shop

BSR Bike Shop in Bangkok provides a fully supported Thai driver's license service — covering the full application process including Thai driver's license, International Driving Permit assistance and document acquisition. All services available in English, Thai and Korean, making BSR particularly useful for Korean expats and visitors who need language support throughout the process. BSR also assists with annual Por Ror Bor renewals and road tax for riders based in Bangkok.

Website: bsrbikeshop.com

Google Maps: View on Google Maps

Location: Phra Khanong, Bangkok

Chiang Mai — Chiang Mai Visa Run

Chiang Mai Visa Run is the most established and well-reviewed license assistance service in the north, specialising in Thai licence applications and conversions for foreigners. The team handles document preparation, DLT appointments and the full application process — including residence certificates which can be one of the more time-consuming elements to arrange independently. Highly regarded within the Chiang Mai expat community for reliable, English-friendly service.

Website: chiangmaivisarun.com

Location: Chiang Mai

Phuket & Pattaya — TDL Service

TDL Service is Thailand's most established national driving license service for foreigners, operating across Bangkok, Chiang Mai, Phuket and Pattaya. With over 4,000 clients from more than 40 countries and a 98% first-attempt pass rate, TDL handles the full license conversion process with English-speaking staff. Services include new license applications, license conversions, renewals, and Por Ror Bor and road tax renewals across all locations.

Website: tdl-service.com

Locations: Phuket and Pattaya

Should You Use an Assistance Service?

For riders and drivers who are confident with paperwork and have time to navigate the DLT system independently, the DIY route is entirely feasible. The DLT process has become more foreigner-friendly in recent years and English is available for the theory exam and at most major offices.

Where assistance services genuinely earn their fee is on the document preparation side — particularly the residence certificate, which can require a visit to the Immigration Office and is the element most likely to cause delays or complications for first-time applicants. Assistance services also commonly have access to express appointment queues — significantly reducing what can otherwise be a wait of several weeks to months at busy DLT offices.
